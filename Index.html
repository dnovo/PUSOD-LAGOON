The Lagoon

A Hidden Paradise

Pusod is a pristine private lagoon nestled within the dramatic karst landscape of Coron Island. Accessible only by boat, this secluded sanctuary offers crystal-clear waters in breathtaking shades of emerald and turquoise, surrounded by ancient limestone formations draped in lush tropical foliage.

Our exclusive day tours provide an intimate experience with nature — limited to a select number of guests each day, ensuring you enjoy the lagoon in its most pristine and undisturbed state.
